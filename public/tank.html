🧻 The Paper Towel Challenge

Watch water "walk" uphill between the cups — that's capillary action!
  1. Fill the outer cups with colored water (red + blue).
  2. Bridge each cup with a folded paper towel strip.
  3. Water climbs the towel and drips into the empty middle cup…
  4. …mixing into purple. No pouring required! 💜
